Tactical Intelligence

Welcome Commander. The mission is simple: occupy more territory than your opponent.

  • Player 1 starts at the TOP-LEFT.
  • Player 2 starts at the BOTTOM-RIGHT.
  • On your turn, roll the dices to get the dimensions of your new sector.
  • You can flip the dimensions (3x2 vs 2x3).
  • New sectors MUST be adjacent to your existing territory.
  • If you cannot place a sector, you must pass.
