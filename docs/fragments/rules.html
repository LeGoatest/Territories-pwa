Rules of Engagement

1. Objective

Control all territories on the board or have the highest territory count when the game ends.

2. Phases

  • Reinforce: Click on your territory to add troops.
  • Attack: (Coming soon) Engage adjacent enemy territories.
  • Fortify: (Coming soon) Move troops between adjacent territories.

3. Turn Order

Players take turns in sequence. A full round is complete when all players have finished their turn.
